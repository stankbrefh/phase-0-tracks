Muay Thai

A Brief Overview

Muay Thai is a combat sport of Thailand that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This physical and mental discipline, which includes combat on shins, is known as "the art of eight limbs." It is characterized by the combined use of:

  • Fists
  • Elbows
  • Knees
  • Shins
